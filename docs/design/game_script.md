## Gameplay Loop

- **Scene:** The player is presented with a computer screen, an old-fashioned terminal interface.
- **Choice:** The player is prompted with a choice:
    - `> connect`
    - `> do not connect`
- **Branching:**
    - **If `do not connect`:**
        - The screen fades to black.
        - **Text:** "You are already connected."
        - **Ending:** Bad End.
    - **If `connect`:**
        - The player enters the Wired.
        - The gameplay is divided into "layers".
        - The player can navigate through different layers to find information about Lain, interact with other characters, and unlock new layers.
        - The player's choices and actions will influence the narrative and lead to different endings.

### Layers of the Wired

- **Layer 01: The Surface Web** - Publicly available information, news sites, forums, etc.
- **Layer 02: The Social Web** - Social media, blogs, personal websites, private messages.
- **Layer 03: The Warez Scene** - Hacking, cracking, pirated software, and other illegal activities.
- **Layer 04: The Deep Web** - Hidden services, anonymous networks, secret communities.
- **Layer 05: The Knights of the Eastern Calculus** - The secret society that claims to be the "God of the Wired". The player can find information about their members, their goals, and their secrets.
- **Layer 06: The Collective Unconscious** - The realm of pure information, where the boundaries between the physical and digital worlds blur. The player can find the "truth" about Lain and the nature of reality.
- **Layer 07: God** - The ultimate layer of the Wired, where the player can meet "God" and ask about the meaning of life, the universe, and everything.

## Endings

- **Ending A: The Wired God** - The player merges with the Wired and becomes its new god, but loses their physical body and human emotions.
- **Ending B: The Lonely God** - The player becomes a god, but is worshipped by no one and is utterly alone.
- **Ending C: The Reset** - The player resets the world, erasing everyone's memories of Lain, including their own.
- **Ending D: The Real World** - The player rejects the Wired and returns to the real world, but loses all their memories of the Wired and Lain.
- **Ending E: The Love** - The player finds love and companionship in the real world, and learns to live with the memories of the Wired.

## Characters

- **Lain Iwakura:** The protagonist of the story. A shy, introverted girl who becomes deeply involved in the Wired.
- **Alice Mizuki:** Lain's best and only friend. A kind and caring girl who tries to keep Lain grounded in the real world.
- **Reika Yamamoto:** A classmate of Lain's.
- **Julie Kato:** A classmate of Lain's.
- **Taro:** A young boy who is a member of the Knights of the Eastern Calculus.
- **The Knights of the Eastern Calculus:** A secret society of hackers who worship the "God of the Wired".
- **Eiri Masami:** The creator of Protocol 7 and the self-proclaimed "God of the Wired".
